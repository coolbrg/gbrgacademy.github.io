What is DevOps?

a combination of software development (Dev) and information technology operations (Ops). DevOps is a set of software development practices that aim to shorten the systems development life cycle while delivering features, fixes, and updates frequently in close alignment with business objectives.

What is the need for DevOps?

  • Shorter Development Cycles, Faster Innovation
  • Reduced Deployment Failures, Rollbacks, and Time to Recover
  • Improved Communication and Collaboration
  • Increased Efficiencies
  • Reduced Costs and IT Headcount
  • Read/Write Files
  • Regular Expression
  • Class & Ojbect
  • Inheritance
  • Duck Typing
  • Module or Mixins
  • Exceptions
